# Knight TV font

`knighttv.ttf` is the character-generator font of the MIT AI Lab's Knight TV
terminals (the displays that fronted the ITS operating system). MIT released it
under the GNU General Public License in 1999.

- Source / upstream: https://github.com/larsbrinkhoff/Knight-TV-font
- Licence: GNU GPL v3 or later (see `COPYING`)
- Source form (for GPL compliance): `knighttv.bdf`

Used on this site for the SHRDLU wordmark, in keeping with the program's home on
ITS at the MIT AI Lab.
