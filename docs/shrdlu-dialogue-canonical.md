# SHRDLU — the canonical demonstration dialogue

Verbatim from Terry Winograd's own SHRDLU page ("The dialog that was used as a
SHRDLU demo"), via Wayback capture 2020-08-17 of `hci.stanford.edu/winograd/shrdlu/`.
Original typos and spacing quirks (e.g. "ANWHERE") are PRESERVED — they are evidence
of the demo's status as a hand-scripted artifact and are of critical interest.

Person types in lowercase; SHRDLU ("Computer") replies in upper case. Parenthetical
"(does it)" are Winograd's own stage directions.

1. Person: PICK UP A BIG RED BLOCK.
   Computer: OK. (does it)
2. Person: GRASP THE PYRAMID.
   Computer: I DON'T UNDERSTAND WHICH PYRAMID YOU MEAN.
3. Person: FIND A BLOCK WHICH IS TALLER THAN THE ONE YOU ARE HOLDING AND PUT IT INTO THE BOX.
   Computer: BY "IT", I ASSUME YOU MEAN THE BLOCK WHICH IS TALLER THAN THE ONE I AM HOLDING.
   Computer: OK. (does it)
4. Person: WHAT DOES THE BOX CONTAIN?
   Computer: THE BLUE PYRAMID AND THE BLUE BLOCK.
5. Person: WHAT IS THE PYRAMID SUPPORTED BY?
   Computer: THE BOX.
6. Person: HOW MANY BLOCKS ARE NOT IN THE BOX?
   Computer: FOUR OF THEM.
7. Person: IS AT LEAST ONE OF THEM NARROWER THAN THE ONE WHICH I TOLD YOU TO PICK UP?
   Computer: YES, THE RED CUBE.
8. Person: IS IT SUPPORTED?
   Computer: YES, BY THE TABLE.
9. Person: CAN THE TABLE PICK UP BLOCKS?
   Computer: NO.
10. Person: CAN A PYRAMID BE SUPPORTED BY A BLOCK?
    Computer: YES.
11. Person: CAN A PYRAMID SUPPORT A PYRAMID?
    Computer: I DON'T KNOW.
12. Person: STACK UP TWO PYRAMIDS.
    Computer: I CAN'T.
13. Person: WILL YOU PLEASE STACK UP BOTH OF THE RED BLOCKS AND EITHER A GREEN CUBE OR A PYRAMID?
    Computer: OK. (does it)
14. Person: WHICH CUBE IS SITTING ON THE TABLE?
    Computer: THE LARGE GREEN ONE WHICH SUPPORTS THE RED PYRAMID.
15. Person: IS THERE A LARGE BLOCK BEHIND A PYRAMID?
    Computer: YES, THREE OF THEM: A LARGE RED ONE, A LARGE GREEN CUBE, AND THE BLUE ONE.
16. Person: PUT A SMALL ONE ONTO THE GREEN CUBE WHICH SUPPORTS A PYRAMID.
    Computer: OK. (does it)
17. Person: PUT THE LITTLEST PYRAMID ON TOP OF IT.
    Computer: OK. (does it)
18. Person: DOES THE SHORTEST THING THE TALLEST PYRAMID'S SUPPORT SUPPORTS SUPPORT ANYTHING GREEN?
    Computer: YES, THE GREEN PYRAMID.
19. Person: WHAT COLOR IS IT?
    Computer: BY "IT", I ASSUME YOU MEAN THE SHORTEST THING THE TALLEST PYRAMID'S SUPPORT SUPPORTS.
    Computer: RED
20. Person: HOW MANY THINGS ARE ON TOP OF GREEN CUBES?
    Computer: I'M NOT SURE WHAT YOU MEAN BY "ON TOP OF" IN THE PHRASE "ON TOP OF GREEN CUBES".
    DO YOU MEAN:
    1 - DIRECTLY ON THE SURFACE
    2 - ANWHERE ON TOP OF ?
    Person: 2
    Computer: THREE OF THEM.
21. Person: HAD YOU TOUCHED ANY PYRAMID BEFORE YOU PUT THE GREEN ONE ON THE LITTLE CUBE?
    Computer: YES, THE GREEN ONE.
22. Person: WHEN DID YOU PICK IT UP?
    Computer: WHILE I WAS STACKING UP THE RED CUBE, A LARGE RED BLOCK AND A LARGE GREEN CUBE.
23. Person: WHY?
    Computer: TO GET RID OF IT.
24. Person: WHY DID YOU DO THAT?
    Computer: TO CLEAN OFF THE RED CUBE.
25. Person: WHY DID YOU CLEAR OFF THAT CUBE?
    Computer: TO PUT IT ON A LARGE GREEN CUBE.
26. Person: WHY DID YOU DO THAT?
    Computer: TO STACK UP THE RED CUBE, A LARGE RED BLOCK AND A LARGE GREEN CUBE.
27. Person: WHY DID YOU DO THAT?
    Computer: BECAUSE YOU ASKED ME TO.
28. Person: HOW DID YOU DO IT?
    Computer: BY PUTTING A LARGE RED BLOCK ON THE TABLE; THEN LETTING GO OF IT; THEN PUTTING A LARGE GREEN CUBE ON IT; THEN LETTING GO OF THAT CUBE; THEN PUTTING THE RED CUBE ON THAT CUBE; THEN LETTING GO OF THAT CUBE.
29. Person: HOW MANY OBJECTS DID YOU TOUCH WHILE YOU WERE DOING IT?
    Computer: FOUR OF THEM.
30. Person: WHAT DID THE RED CUBE SUPPORT BEFORE YOU STARTED TO CLEAN IT OFF?
    Computer: THE GREEN PYRAMID.
31. Person: THERE WERE FIVE BLOCKS TO THE LEFT OF THE BOX THEN.
    Computer: NO, ONLY FOUR OF THEM: THE RED CUBE, TWO LARGE GREEN CUBES AND A LARGE RED BLOCK.
32. Person: PUT THE BLUE PYRAMID ON THE BLOCK IN THE BOX.
    Computer: OK. (does it)
33. Person: IS THERE ANYTHING WHICH IS BIGGER THAN EVERY PYRAMID BUT IS NOT AS WIDE AS THE THING THAT SUPPORTS IT?
    Computer: YES, THE BLUE BLOCK.
34. Person: DOES A STEEPLE
    Computer (interrupting): SORRY, I DON'T KNOW THE WORD "STEEPLE".
35. Person: A "STEEPLE" IS A STACK WHICH CONTAINS TWO GREEN CUBES AND A PYRAMID.
    Computer: I UNDERSTAND.
36. Person: ARE THERE ANY STEEPLES NOW?
    Computer: NO.
37. Person: BUILD ONE.
    Computer: OK. (does it)
38. Person: CALL THE BIGGEST BLOCK "SUPERBLOCK".
    Computer: OK.
39. Person: HAVE YOU PICKED SUPERBLOCK UP SINCE WE BEGAN?
    Computer: YES.
40. Person: WHY DID YOU DROP IT?
    Computer: BY "IT", I ASSUME YOU MEAN THE SUPERBLOCK.
    I CAN'T EXPLAIN A NON-EXISTENT EVENT.
41. Person: IS THERE ANYTHING TO THE RIGHT OF THE RED PYRAMID?
    Computer: YES, FOUR THINGS: THE BOX, THE RED CUBE, THE BLUE PYRAMID AND THE BLOCK NAMED SUPERBLOCK.
42. Person: THANK YOU.
    Computer: YOU'RE WELCOME!
</content>
