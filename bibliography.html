Bibliography

These are the works this reading of SHRDLU draws on: Terry Winograd's own writings, the technical infrastructure on which the program was built, the critique that grew up around it and Winograd's own later turn, the histories and secondary scholarship, the Critical Code Studies method, and the surviving reconstructions of the code. Citations are being verified against the project's reference library; where a date or detail remains uncertain, that is noted rather than guessed.
